Stabilization

 Top  Previous  Next

Even when a user intends for his or her hand to remain stationary, there are sometime small hand movements or “jitters” that your application should usually ignore. It is likely that your application should neither respond to these small movements nor display them, if the hand is displayed.

The stabilizer feature causes the tracking to automatically ignore such small jitters and represent the relevant tracking data as if the hand did not move at all, producing a better user experience.

See EnableStabilizer.
