Description

The CoordinateSystem enumerator itemizes supported camera coordinate systems. Use the bit-OR’ed values to represent groups of coordinate systems.

Name/Description

 

COORDINATE_SYSTEM_FRONT_DEFAULT

 

 

From the camera view, X points to the right, Y up, and Z towards the user. The origin is at the center of the depth camera.

 

 

COORDINATE_SYSTEM_REAR_DEFAULT

 

 

The axis X points to the right, Y up and Z towards the user. The origin is at the center of the depth camera.

 

 

COORDINATE_SYSTEM_REAR_OPENCV

 

 

The axis X points to the right, Y down, and Z towards the scene. The origin is at the center of the depth camera.
