Accessing Hand Tracking Status

 Top  Previous  Next

The QueryTrackingStatus function returns a bit-mask to indicate the status of the tracking data. Its value is either “good” (a zero value), or a bit-wise OR of one or more tracking problem values, indicating that the tracking data may not be reliable. You may choose to ignore any tracking data that’s produced when the tracking status is not “good”.

The tracking status is meaningful only in full-hand tracking mode.

The tracking status is a bit-wise combination of the following TrackingStatusType flags:

Enumeration Name

Reason for accuracy loss

TRACKING_STATUS_GOOD

The tracking data is reliable

TRACKING_STATUS_OUT_OF_FOV

The hand is approaching FOV edges

TRACKING_STATUS_OUT_OF_RANGE

The hand is approaching minimal/maximal distance from the sensor

TRACKING_STATUS_HIGH_SPEED

The hand is moving at a high speed

TRACKING_STATUS_POINTING_FINGER

The fingers are pointing towards the sensor
