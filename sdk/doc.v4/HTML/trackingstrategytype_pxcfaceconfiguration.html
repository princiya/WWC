Description

The TrackingStrategyType enumerator enumerates the face tracking modes.

Name/Description

 

STRATEGY_APPEARANCE_TIME

Track faces based on their appearance in the scene.

 

STRATEGY_CLOSEST_TO_FARTHEST

Track faces from the closest to the furthest.

 

STRATEGY_FARTHEST_TO_CLOSEST

Track faces from the furthest to the closest.

 

STRATEGY_LEFT_TO_RIGHT

Track faces from left to right.

 

STRATEGY_RIGHT_TO_LEFT

Track faces from right to left.
