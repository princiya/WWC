Description

The AlertType enumerator itemizes the alert messages.

Name/Description

 

LABEL_VOLUME_HIGH

The volume is too high for recognition.

 

LABEL_VOLUME_LOW

The volume is too low for recognition.

 

LABEL_SNR_LOW

The signal-to-noise ratio is too low for recognition.

 

LABEL_SPEECH_UNRECOGNIZABLE

The speech is detected but not recognized.

 

LABEL_SPEECH_BEGIN

The speech is detected and starting.

 

LABEL_SPEECH_END

The speech is detected and ended.

 

LABEL_RECOGNITION_ABORTED

The recognition is aborted due to error such as the engine internal errors or audio source failure.

 

LABEL_RECOGNITION_ENDED

The recognition is completed. The audio source no longer provides any data.
