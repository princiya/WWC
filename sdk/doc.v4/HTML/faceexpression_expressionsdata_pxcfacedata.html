Description

The FaceExpression enumerator enumerates supported face expressions.

Name/Description

Eye brow Movement

EXPRESSION_BROW_RAISER_LEFT

The left eye-brow is raised up.

EXPRESSION_BROW_RAISER_RIGHT

The right eyebrow is raised up.

EXPRESSION_BROW_LOWERER_LEFT

The left eyebrow is lowered.

EXPRESSION_BROW_LOWERER_RIGHT

The right eyebrow is lowered.

Mouth Movement

EXPRESSION_SMILE

The face is smiling.

EXPRESSION_KISS

The mouth lips are raised for a kiss action.

EXPRESSION_MOUTH_OPEN

The mouth is opened.

 

EXPRESSION_TONGUE_OUT

The tongue is out of the mouth.

Head Movement

EXPRESSION_HEAD_TURN_LEFT

The head turned left.

EXPRESSION_HEAD_TURN_RIGHT

The head turned right.

EXPRESSION_HEAD_UP

The head turned up.

EXPRESSION_HEAD_DOWN

The head turned down

EXPRESSION_HEAD_TILT_LEFT

The head is tilted to the left.

EXPRESSION_HEAD_TILT_RIGHT

The head is tilted to the right.

Eye Movement

EXPRESSION_EYES_CLOSED_LEFT

The left eye is closed.

EXPRESSION_EYES_CLOSED_RIGHT

The right eye is closed.

EXPRESSION_EYES_TURN_LEFT

The eyes are turning left.

EXPRESSION_EYES_TURN_RIGHT

The eyes are turning right.

EXPRESSION_EYES_UP

The eyes are turning up.

EXPRESSION_EYES_DOWN

The eyes are turning down.

EXPRESSION_PUFF_LEFT

The mouth blows air to the left.

EXPRESSION_PUFF_RIGHT

The mouth blows air to the right.
