Description

The LandmarksGroupType enumerator enumerates supported face landmark groups.

Name/Description

 

LANDMARK_GROUP_LEFT_EYE

The landmark group for retrieving the left eye points.

 

LANDMARK_GROUP_RIGHT_EYE

The landmark group for retrieving the right eye points.

 

LANDMARK_GROUP_MOUTH

The landmark group for retrieving the mouth points.

 

LANDMARK_GROUP_NOSE

The landmark group for retrieving the nose points.

 

LANDMARK_GROUP_LEFT_EYEBROW

The landmark group for retrieving the left eye brow points.

 

LANDMARK_GROUP_RIGHT_EYEBROW

The landmark group for retrieving the right eye brow points.

 

LANDMARK_GROUP_JAW

The landmark group for retrieving the jaw points.
