Description

The ExtremityType enumerator itemizes the blob extremity point labels.

Name/Description

 

EXTREMITY_CLOSEST

The closest point to the camera of the blob.

 

EXTREMITY_LEFTMOST

The left most point of the blob.

 

EXTREMITY_RIGHTMOST

The right most point of the blob.

 

EEXTREMITY_TOPMOST

The top most point of the blob.

 

EXTREMITY_BOTTOMMOST

The bottom most point of the blob.

 

EXTREMITY_CENTER

The central point of the blob.
